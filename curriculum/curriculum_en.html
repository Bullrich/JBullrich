PERSONAL SUMMARY

I'm Javier Bullrich, years old, videogame development student, gamer, bartender and web & game developer.

At my 19s I studied Game Design under the guidance of Durgan A. Nallar. Upon completion of the course I discovered I had a bigger passion to create games than to play them and decided to enter the industry.

I consider myself a motivating, creative and cheerful person, always trying to learn as much as possible and contribute my knowledge. I'm a bit competitive, I try to take the initiative and to stand out in what I do.

I have a couple of games in the Play Store, I was lead programmer in the game Splashy Cats, which got to the eighteen most downloaded free game in the AppStore with 2.000.000 downloads.

I write a lot in my free time, I have a blog and I'm also studying literature.

The goal of my life is to work making video games, and, one day in the future found my own bar

I have no problems in moving to another country
