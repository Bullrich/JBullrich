Mother Love

Mother Love is a game in which your mother is dying, and she ask you to hold her hand one last time.
How much time will you be able to make her happy before your anxiety makes you abandon her?
Inspired on this tweet of @PeterMolydeux

The development

This game was developed with HaxeFlixel using FlashDevelop IDE. It's coded in Haxe.
